Peruvian Street Food – Site Plan

A website project plan for WDD 131

Site Name

Peruvian Street Food

This name highlights the authentic flavors of Peru’s street cuisine. It was chosen because it is simple, memorable, and directly relates to the cultural food experience the site will showcase.

Optional domain: perustreetfood.org

Site Purpose

The website aims to introduce visitors to the vibrant world of Peruvian street food. It will provide recipes, cultural background, photos of popular dishes, and information on where to try authentic street food. It will also include a contact form for food enthusiasts and a subscription option for updates.

Scenarios

  • Where can I learn how to prepare popular Peruvian street food dishes like anticuchos and picarones?
  • What are the cultural traditions behind Peruvian street food?
  • How can I find authentic Peruvian street food vendors in Lima?

Color Schema

Selected colors are inspired by Peruvian culture and cuisine.

  • #B22222 (Firebrick Red) – Used for headers and accents.
  • #FFD700 (Golden Yellow) – Used for highlights, buttons, and icons.
  • #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) – Used for backgrounds.

Typography

  • Montserrat (Sans-serif) – Used for headings and navigation.
  • Roboto (Sans-serif) – Used for body text for readability.
